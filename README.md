# AI Lead Qualification & Automated Outreach System

An end-to-end AI-powered workflow that automatically researches, scores, and prioritizes incoming leads — and generates personalized outreach emails inside your CRM in seconds.

## 🚀 Overview

Sales teams waste hours manually researching leads, qualifying them, and writing outreach emails.

This system eliminates that work completely.

The moment a new lead is added to the CRM, the system:
- Analyzes the company
- Scores the lead
- Classifies priority
- Generates a personalized email

All in under **12 seconds** — with zero human effort.

---

## ⚡ Key Features

- **Automated Lead Detection**
- **AI-Based Website Research**
- **Lead Scoring (0–100)**
- **HOT / WARM / COLD Classification**
- **Personalized Email Generation**
- **CRM Auto-Update**
- **Airtable Logging & Dashboard**

---

## 🧠 How It Works

### Step 1 — Detection
A webhook instantly captures new contacts from the CRM including:
- Name
- Email
- Company
- Website

---

### Step 2 — Website Research
The system visits the company website and extracts meaningful business information.

---

### Step 3 — AI Analysis
Using **GPT-4o-mini**, the system analyzes:
- Industry
- Company size
- Pain points
- Automation opportunities
- Lead score (0–100)

---

### Step 4 — Classification

| Score Range | Category | Action |
|------------|--------|--------|
| 70–100     | HOT    | Immediate outreach |
| 40–69      | WARM   | Follow-up |
| 0–39       | COLD   | Drip campaign |

---

### Step 5 — CRM Update

The system automatically:
- Tags the lead (HOT/WARM/COLD)
- Adds a **personalized email draft** to the contact timeline

---

### Step 6 — Data Logging

All lead data is stored in Airtable:
- Score
- Industry
- Pain points
- Classification
- Timestamp

---

### Step 7 — Dashboard

A live dashboard provides:
- Total leads processed
- HOT / WARM / COLD distribution
- Average lead score
- Industry breakdown
